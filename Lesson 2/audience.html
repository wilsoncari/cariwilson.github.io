Target Audience

Our target audience will be individuals and groups who either want to plan a visit or are thinking of moving to Idaho. More specifically men and women ages 20-45 years of age. This age group will typically have families who want to bring their kids on a vacation or individuals looking for a place to get away. This age group is also the most likely to be looking to move, especially young couples looking for a less expensive place to start out.

Personas

1. Young (20-30 year olds) men and women who love the outdoors and are looking for a quick getaway to hike, camp, fish, and site see.
2. Parents (ages 25-45) with kids who are looking for a fun outdoorsy vacation or a great place to raise a family.

Scenarios

  • A family looking for an inexpensive place to relocate.
  • Parent who want a family friendly environment to raise their kids.
  • Any size family looking for a place to hold a reunion or vacation.
  • A young couple looking for their first place to call home.
  • Young adventurers looking for an outodoor getaway.
