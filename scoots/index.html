Ride With us!

Cozumel is the largest Mexican Caribbean island. It’s white sandy beaches, historic Mayan relics, and natural beauty make it the perfect destination spot. And there’s no better way to take in the sights than on a scooter from Scoots Rental Co.

We have two great locations near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel and the other near the Terminal Puerta, which caters to the cruise lines and patrons. With rentals from 2-6 hours, you’ll be able to see and do more. Go shopping, take a tour of the city, or ride along the coastline. You can’t go wrong with Scoots.
