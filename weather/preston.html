Preston is Famous!

By The Crazy Tourist

Before the hit movie “Napoleon Dynamite,” Preston was an unknown blip on a map to most of America – and the rest of the world. But when Preston resident Jared Hess showcased Preston as being a quirky, lovable, and fun small town, fans came running. The town took advantage by being the premier site to sell “Vote for Pedro” shirts, tater tots, and chapstick. Today, Napoleon’s legacy has worn off slightly but Preston has managed to retain its feel-good atmosphere. Even if you’re not a fan of the quirky film, you should stop to enjoy the nearby natural parks like the Glendale Reservoir and other lakes.
